Title: Colorspace
----

Year: 2013

----

Tags: space

----
Teaser: Experiments with Colour and ight in an architectural room
----

Text: "Colorspace" is a project about light, colour and architecture. Our aim was to create an awareness of a chosen area. Through accurate illumination of the room made by light-mapping and specific colours which we thought was suitable, we created various atmospheres in the selected space.
